Designer Timber Furniture Store retails fine quality, modern and traditional furniture at the lowest prices.

We provide exceptional customer service and deliver on our promise of “Affordable Luxury” at guaranteed lower prices.

We are a Retail Outlet in Melbourne for major furniture wholesalers stocking a range of Australian and European designs.

We specialise in:
  • Living
  • Dining
  • Bedroom
  • Outdoor
